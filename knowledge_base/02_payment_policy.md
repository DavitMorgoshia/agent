# Payment Terms Policy — Vendor Contracts

**Policy ID:** FIN-PAY-002 | **Version:** 3.1 | **Effective:** 2024-01-01
**Owner:** Finance & Procurement | **Approved by:** CFO

---

## 1. Standard Payment Terms

### 1.1 Acceptable Terms
- **Net-30** is the company standard for all vendor payments.
- **Net-45** is acceptable for contracts over $250,000/year with CFO pre-approval.
- **Advance payments** (payment before delivery) require CFO approval for amounts over $10,000.
- **Milestone-based payments** are acceptable for project-based engagements.

### 1.2 Non-Compliant Terms (Flag for Review)
- **Net-60 or longer**: Not acceptable without CFO exception. Flag all Net-60+ contracts.
- **Net-15 or shorter**: Creates cash flow risk. Requires Finance review.
- **Payment on receipt / immediate**: Only acceptable for amounts under $1,000.
- **Non-standard schedules**: Any payment schedule not listed above requires Finance sign-off.

---

## 2. Contract Value Thresholds

| Value Range | Authority | Payment Approval |
|---|---|---|
| Under $10,000 | Department Head | Standard Net-30 |
| $10,000 – $50,000 | VP Level | Standard Net-30 |
| $50,000 – $250,000 | CFO | Net-30 or approved Net-45 |
| Over $250,000 | Board / C-Suite | Special approval required |

---

## 3. Currency Policy
- USD is the default billing currency.
- EUR, GBP, CAD are acceptable with FX hedging for contracts over $50,000.
- Non-major currencies require Treasury approval.
- Contracts must specify currency conversion mechanism for multi-currency arrangements.

---

## 4. Late Payment / Penalty Clauses
- Vendor-imposed late fees above 1.5%/month must be negotiated down or removed.
- Suspension-of-service clauses with less than 30 days' grace period must be reviewed.
- Interest rates above prime + 2% are not acceptable.

---

## 5. Multi-Year Commitments
- Contracts with annual payment escalation above 5%/year require CFO approval.
- Contracts longer than 3 years require Board approval.
- Price lock-in provisions are encouraged for multi-year agreements.

---

## 6. Prepayment and Upfront Fees
- Annual prepay (paying full year upfront) is acceptable if it results in >5% discount.
- Onboarding/setup fees over $5,000 require department head approval.
- Non-refundable deposits over $10,000 require CFO sign-off.
